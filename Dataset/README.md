## Feature Definitions and Formulas for Fundamental Analysis of the Iranian Stock Market Dataset

The dataset includes the following columns:

### 1. **CompanyId**
   - **Definition**: A unique identifier for each company in the dataset.

### 2. **PersianYear**
   - **Definition**: The financial year according to the Persian calendar.

### 3. **Return**
   - **Definition**: The gain or loss on an investment over a period.
   - **Formula**: (Ending Value - Beginning Value + Dividends) / Beginning Value * 100

### 4. **Beta**
   - **Definition**: A measure of a stock's volatility compared to the market.
   - **Formula**: Covariance(Return of Stock, Return of Market) / Variance of Market Return

### 5. **Debt to Total Assets Ratio**
   - **Definition**: The proportion of assets financed by debt.
   - **Formula**: Total Debt / Total Assets * 100

### 6. **Operating Profit to Sale**
   - **Definition**: Efficiency of core business operations.
   - **Formula**: Operating Profit / Net Sales * 100

### 7. **Fixed Asset Turnover**
   - **Definition**: Efficiency in using fixed assets to generate sales.
   - **Formula**: Net Sales / Average Fixed Assets

### 8. **Return from Market**
   - **Definition**: The return generated by the market.
   - **Formula**: (Market Ending Value - Market Beginning Value) / Market Beginning Value * 100

### 9. **Current Assets Ratio**
   - **Definition**: Indicates liquidity by comparing current assets to total assets.
   - **Formula**: Current Assets / Total Assets * 100

### 10. **Working Capital Return**
    - **Definition**: Return generated by working capital.
    - **Formula**: Net Sales / Working Capital * 100

### 11. **Long-term Debt to Equity Ratio**
    - **Definition**: Ratio of long-term debt to shareholders' equity.
    - **Formula**: Long-term Debt / Equity * 100

### 12. **Return Ratio Without Risk**
    - **Definition**: Return on an investment excluding risk (risk-free rate).
    - **Formula**: Risk-Free Rate of Return

### 13. **Profit Margin Growth Rate**
    - **Definition**: The growth rate of profit margin over time.
    - **Formula**: (Current Period Profit Margin - Previous Period Profit Margin) / Previous Period Profit Margin * 100

### 14. **Stock Market Value**
    - **Definition**: Total market value of a company's shares.
    - **Formula**: Share Price * Total Number of Shares Outstanding

### 15. **Net Profit to Sale**
    - **Definition**: Ratio of net profit to net sales.
    - **Formula**: Net Profit / Net Sales * 100

### 16. **Net Profit to Gross Profit**
    - **Definition**: Ratio of net profit to gross profit.
    - **Formula**: Net Profit / Gross Profit * 100

### 17. **Current Ratio**
    - **Definition**: Measures ability to pay short-term obligations.
    - **Formula**: Current Assets / Current Liabilities

### 18. **Net Working Capital**
    - **Definition**: The difference between current assets and liabilities.
    - **Formula**: Current Assets - Current Liabilities

### 19. **Operating Assets Ratio**
    - **Definition**: Ratio of operating assets to total assets.
    - **Formula**: Operating Assets / Total Assets * 100

### 20. **Total Asset Turnover**
    - **Definition**: Efficiency in using assets to generate sales.
    - **Formula**: Net Sales / Average Total Assets

### 21. **DPS (Dividends Per Share)**
    - **Definition**: Dividend paid per share.
    - **Formula**: Total Dividends Paid / Number of Outstanding Shares

### 22. **EPS Growth**
    - **Definition**: Growth rate of earnings per share (EPS).
    - **Formula**: (Current Period EPS - Previous Period EPS) / Previous Period EPS * 100

### 23. **Liquidity Ratios**
    - **Definition**: Ratios that measure the ability to cover short-term obligations.
    - **Formula**: Includes Current Ratio and Quick Ratio.

### 24. **ROA (Return on Assets)**
    - **Definition**: Profitability relative to total assets.
    - **Formula**: Net Income / Total Assets * 100

### 25. **Quick Ratio**
    - **Definition**: Measures ability to meet short-term obligations without relying on inventory.
    - **Formula**: (Current Assets - Inventory) / Current Liabilities

### 26. **Average Payment Period**
    - **Definition**: Average time to pay suppliers.
    - **Formula**: (Accounts Payable * 365) / Cost of Goods Sold

### 27. **ROE (Return on Equity)**
    - **Definition**: Profitability relative to shareholders' equity.
    - **Formula**: Net Income / Shareholders' Equity * 100

### 28. **EPS (Earnings Per Share)**
    - **Definition**: Profit allocated to each outstanding share.
    - **Formula**: (Net Income - Dividends on Preferred Stock) / Average Outstanding Shares

### 29. **Total Income Growth**
    - **Definition**: Growth rate of total income over time.
    - **Formula**: (Current Period Income - Previous Period Income) / Previous Period Income * 100

### 30. **P/E Ratio (Price to Earnings)**
    - **Definition**: Ratio of share price to earnings per share.
    - **Formula**: Share Price / Earnings Per Share (EPS)

### 31. **ROI (Return on Investment)**
    - **Definition**: Gain or loss on an investment relative to the investment cost.
    - **Formula**: Net Profit / Cost of Investment * 100

### 32. **Capital**
    - **Definition**: Total capital, including equity and debt.
    - **Formula**: Equity + Long-term Debt

### 33. **P/B Ratio (Price to Book)**
    - **Definition**: Ratio of market value to book value.
    - **Formula**: Market Price per Share / Book Value per Share

### 34. **P/S Ratio (Price to Sales)**
    - **Definition**: Ratio of stock price to revenues.
    - **Formula**: Market Price per Share / Revenue per Share

### 35. **Cash to Total Assets Ratio**
    - **Definition**: Ratio of cash to total assets.
    - **Formula**: Cash and Cash Equivalents / Total Assets * 100

### 36. **Debt to Equity Ratio**
    - **Definition**: Ratio of total debt to shareholders' equity.
    - **Formula**: Total Debt / Equity * 100

### 37. **Equity Ratio**
    - **Definition**: Ratio of equity to total assets.
    - **Formula**: Equity / Total Assets * 100

### 38. **DuPont Ratio**
    - **Definition**: Breakdown of ROE into profit margin, asset turnover, and financial leverage.
    - **Formula**: ROE = Profit Margin * Asset Turnover * Financial Leverage

### 39. **Gross Profit Margin**
    - **Definition**: Percentage of revenue exceeding the cost of goods sold (COGS).
    - **Formula**: (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue * 100

### 40. **Operating Profit Margin**
    - **Definition**: Percentage of revenue left after operating expenses.
    - **Formula**: Operating Profit / Revenue * 100

### 41. **Net Profit Margin**
    - **Definition**: Percentage of revenue that remains as profit after all expenses.
    - **Formula**: Net Profit / Revenue * 100

### 42. **Sales Growth Rate**
    - **Definition**: Growth rate of sales over time.
    - **Formula**: (Current Period Sales - Previous Period Sales) / Previous Period Sales * 100

### 43. **Current Debt to Total Debt Ratio**
    - **Definition**: Ratio of current debt to total debt.
    - **Formula**: Current Debt / Total Debt * 100

### 44. **Cost to Sales Ratio**
    - **Definition**: Ratio of cost of goods sold to net sales.
    - **Formula**: COGS / Net Sales * 100

### 45. **Sale to Current Assets**
    - **Definition**: Ratio of sales to current assets.
    - **Formula**: Sales / Current Assets * 100

### 46. **Operating Cash Flow to Sales**
    - **Definition**: Ratio of operating cash flow to net sales.
    - **Formula**: Operating Cash Flow / Net Sales * 100

### 47. **Cash Flow Returns to Sales**
    - **Definition**: Ratio of cash flow returns to net sales.
    - **Formula**: Cash Flow / Net Sales * 100

### 48. **Profit Margin Before Interest and Taxes**
    - **Definition**: Ratio of profit before interest and taxes to net sales.
    - **Formula**: Profit Before Interest and Taxes / Net Sales * 100

### 49. **Net Return on Working Capital**
    - **Definition**: Ratio of net profit to working capital.
    - **Formula**: Net Profit / Working Capital * 100
